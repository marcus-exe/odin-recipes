Beef Strogonoff

Description

Just for noticing, the recipe that I am describing is the brazillian version. So naturallt is a bit different from what you may expect, if you are reading this

It's composed of Beef, preferably a not so fat one, milk cream, black pepper, probrably something alcoholic and more stuff. Above all, it must be served with rice and "Batata Palha"

Disclaimer: the next steps aren't related to the brazillian version

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • half teaspoon garlic powder
  • half teaspoon salt
  • half teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 cube beef bouillon
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 8 ounces fresh mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 pint sour cream
  • 4 ounces egg noodles, cooked and drained

Steps

  1. In a large skillet brown beef in oil over medium high heat. When meat is browned, drain excess fat from skillet. Add garlic powder, salt and pepper and stir in.
  2. Add bouillon, onion and mushrooms to skillet and saute until onions are translucent. Remove from heat (very important) and add sour cream. Stir all together and serve over hot cooked egg noodles.
